## Chapter Seven: The Boarding

### The Breach

The armored hull of the *Iron Wake* slammed brutally against the fragile wooden side of the *Gilded Gull*. Magistrate Garret Locke leapt across the churning void between the two ships, landing heavily on the flooded deck. Locke raised his polished sword, his sharp eyes piercing the torrential rain of the gathered storm. The magistrate relentlessly hunted Elara Vance, finally cornering his elusive prey amidst the chaotic, raging ocean. Garret braced his boots against the slippery floorboards, preparing to execute the arrest he had obsessively pursued.

Sergeant Kaelen served his commanding officer with unwavering, absolute dedication throughout this perilous maritime journey. The loyal soldier hurled heavy steel grappling hooks across the gap, permanently tethering the *Gilded Gull* to the imperial warship. Kaelen vaulted over the wooden railing, landing expertly beside Magistrate Garret Locke in a strict defensive stance. The sergeant drew his standard-issue sidearm, scanning the terrified merchant crew for any immediate signs of armed resistance. Sergeant Kaelen silently vowed to protect his superior from any physical dangers lurking aboard the battered vessel.

Captain Jonas Rell refused to surrender the *Gilded Gull* without a fierce, physical confrontation. The veteran sailor commanded his beloved merchant vessel with an iron will, charging forward through the knee-deep saltwater. Captain Jonas Rell swung a heavy boarding axe toward the invading magistrate, roaring a desperate battle cry. Locke smoothly deflected the massive weapon with his sword, twisting the polished steel blade to disarm the furious sailor. Garret struck the captain with the heavy pommel, sending the large man collapsing onto the wave-washed deck.

Elara Vance watched the brutal defeat of her captain while desperately clinging to the wooden mainmast. The smuggler carried the glowing Sunstone Compass tightly against her soaked woolen coat to protect its magical light. Elara Vance realized that escaping the magistrate through sheer physical force was no longer a viable option. Vance held the Sunstone Compass out over the dark, churning water, creating a highly dangerous standoff. The older sister threatened to drop the priceless navigation tool into the freezing ocean if the soldiers advanced another step.

### The Order Exposed

Elira Vance bravely climbed up from the flooded lower cabins to stand beside her older sister. The younger sister tightly gripped her leather satchel, refusing to abandon her only remaining family member. Elira Vance carried the highly contested Black Wax Letter safely tucked away within the waterproof bag. The younger sister also possessed the Obsidian Cipher, fully aware that the heavy stone ring could perfectly translate the treasonous document. Elira glared defiantly at the armed soldiers, masking her absolute terror with impressive sibling solidarity.

Magistrate Garret Locke halted his aggressive advance, staring intensely at the glowing artifact suspended over the deadly waves. Locke pointed his steel blade directly at the chest of the desperate smuggler. The magistrate demanded that Elara Vance hand over the Sunstone Compass and the stolen royal documents immediately. Garret promised that the heavily armored *Iron Wake* would tow the sinking merchant vessel to a safe, imperial harbor. Locke believed that submitting to strict royal justice was the only way these wanted criminals would survive the catastrophic tempest.

Elara Vance ignored the offer, reaching directly into the leather satchel held by her younger sister. The smuggler pulled out the Black Wax Letter, holding the heavy parchment up like a fragile, physical shield. Elara shouted over the howling wind that the encrypted document exposed a massive conspiracy known as the Order of the Silver Cross. Vance stated plainly that the Order of the Silver Cross was a treasonous organization actively plotting to murder the ruling king. The smuggler explicitly warned Garret Locke that his own military superiors proudly belonged to this deeply corrupt organization.

Sergeant Kaelen lowered his brass sidearm slightly, profoundly shocked by the detailed, treasonous accusation. Locke stared at the broken seal of the Black Wax Letter, recognizing the specific political emblems described by the fugitive. The magistrate suddenly understood that the Order of the Silver Cross had secretly orchestrated this entire, deadly pursuit to retrieve their evidence. Garret Locke actively opposed any form of treason, realizing his strict obedience had accidentally served the true traitors. Locke slowly lowered his sword, his rigid worldview violently fracturing under the terrible weight of this ultimate deception.

Elara Vance abruptly tossed the Sunstone Compass through the rain, sending the artifact flying straight toward the conflicted magistrate. Garret caught the glowing device out of pure reflex, staring blankly at its warm, golden light. The smuggler promised to keep the Black Wax Letter and the Obsidian Cipher hidden to ensure the organization remained terrified. Locke nodded grimly, explicitly ordering Sergeant Kaelen to sever the thick grappling hooks connecting the two ships. The magistrate commanded the *Iron Wake* to detach immediately, allowing the *Gilded Gull* to escape safely into the dark horizon.
