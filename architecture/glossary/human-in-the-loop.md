# human-in-the-loop

**Definition:** a design where an automated process *proposes* but a human *commits* the
consequential decision — the machine never finalizes the irreversible step alone.

**Answers:** "who has the final say on a decision the system can't be trusted to get right?"

**First encountered in:** [[invariants]]

This is Story Forge's central product stance: Stage 4 of the cascade is "CRITICAL", every
automatic decision is reversible, and "no UI = no graph data" (§3.3, §11). It is encoded as
[[invariants]] INV-1.
